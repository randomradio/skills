# Consistency Audit  (atomic)

**Purpose:** Score the only thing you control — adherence. Low scores predict blow-ups better than any
chart. Rate each 1–5:

1. Cut losses mechanically; never widened a stop.
2. Let predefined rules — not in-the-moment feelings — drive every decision.
3. Judged myself on execution; treated any single trade's P&L as one sample.
4. Did not overtrade, chase, or try to "make it back".
5. Treated losses as expected, in-budget outcomes and followed the system.
6. Protected the account: risk per trade small and fixed by formula.
7. Specialized — only my 1–2 setups, validated by my own data.
8. Sized up only after evidence; down after losses.
9. Kept it sustainable (traded the high-volatility windows; no screen-staring).
10. Adapted to the current regime instead of forcing a dead one.

**Interpretation:** a week green in dollars but low in adherence is **variance, not skill**, and will
mean-revert. Reward the rules, not the luck.
---

*Scope: process only — not financial advice, no buy/sell calls, no price predictions. Trading is treated as a logical, rule-based system; an urge to deviate is a signal to defer to the plan, never an input. Correct process is necessary, not sufficient: most day traders lose net of costs.*
